PROJECT OVERVIEW

 

MyStatus is the redesign of the Graduate Student Status page in the AccessPlus. AccessPlus is the portal for students to Iowa State University where students can register courses, pay their tuitions and etc. Among them, Graduate Student Status is the place that provides graduate students convenience to check their graduation status, apply for graduation and modify their POS (program of study) forms online. It will be used most often by students who are about or plan to graduate.

The goal of this project is to minimize the problems (will be demonstrated in the next section) of the current system and make it more usable, useful and easier to use. MyStatus will provide a easier and less stressful process than the existing processes offered by the current system in order to help graduate students confidently monitor their graduation plan in their academic program.

This project applies the user-centered design process, through which an iterative design process is initialized based on the requirements gathered in the user research to ensure the design aligns with the demands of target users. Then the design and a digital prototype is developed as well as modification of design based on evaluation.
